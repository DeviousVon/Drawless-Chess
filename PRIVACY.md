# Privacy Policy for Drawless Chess

**Effective date:** July 11, 2026
**Last updated:** July 14, 2026

BB_Games ("we," "us," or "our") publishes Drawless Chess, Android package
`com.drawlesschess`. This policy explains how the app handles information.

## Privacy at a glance

Drawless Chess is an offline, single-player chess game. The app:

- does not require an account or sign-in;
- does not collect or transmit personal information to BB_Games;
- does not contain advertising, analytics, tracking, crash-reporting, or in-app
  purchase SDKs; and
- does not request network, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or shared-storage
  permissions.

## Information kept on your device

To provide gameplay and Resume, Drawless Chess stores app data locally in Android's
private app storage. This can include:

- the current or most recently saved game, board position, moves, result, clock state,
  chosen rules, player side, opponent level, and counts of gameplay actions such as
  hints, undos, and pauses;
- a random device-local player identifier and completed-game history used to calculate
  wins, losses, streaks, unassisted wins, and average game score; and
- app preferences, including the selected visual theme and whether the introductory
  rules guide has been dismissed.

This information is used only to run the game, restore a saved game, show local player
statistics, and remember your preferences. The local player identifier is not an account,
is not linked to an online identity, and is not sent to BB_Games or to an in-app
third-party service.

## Android backup

Drawless Chess allows Android's system backup feature. Depending on your device, account,
and backup settings, Android or your device provider may include the locally stored game
and preference data described above in a device or cloud backup. That backup is performed
and controlled by the operating-system or backup provider, not by Drawless Chess.
BB_Games does not receive, access, or control the backed-up data.

You can manage or delete device backups through your Android device and Google account
backup settings. The exact controls and retention period are set by your device or backup
provider.

## Network access and external services

Drawless Chess has no Android internet permission and does not directly send gameplay or
preference data over the internet. The Open-source license and Privacy screens include
fixed links to the project's public source repository and this policy. If you choose one
of those links, Android opens it in an external browser or another app you select. That
external app and GitHub process the visit under their own privacy policies; Drawless Chess
does not receive information about it.

If you install or purchase Drawless Chess through Google Play, Google processes store,
download, transaction, device, and related information as the platform provider. BB_Games
does not receive your payment-card or bank details. Google may provide developers with
Play Console reports such as aggregate installation statistics and platform-generated
performance or crash diagnostics. Those Play services are governed by Google's terms and
privacy policy and are not data transmitted by Drawless Chess itself.

## Sharing and sale of information

Drawless Chess does not transmit personal information or app activity to BB_Games, so we
do not sell it or share it with third parties. The app has no advertising network, user
profiling, cross-app tracking, or developer-operated server.

## Retention and deletion

An in-progress saved game remains on the device until it is completed, forfeited, or
cleared with the app's data. Completed-game history, local statistics, and preferences
remain until the app's data is cleared or the app is uninstalled.
To delete the completed-game history and all other data stored by the installed app, use
Android **Settings > Apps > Drawless Chess > Storage > Clear storage/data**, or uninstall
Drawless Chess.

Clearing app data or uninstalling may not immediately remove a separate Android or cloud
backup. Manage those copies through your device or Google account backup settings. Because
Drawless Chess has no user accounts or developer-operated data store, BB_Games has no
server-side account or gameplay record to locate or delete.

## Children

Drawless Chess is not specifically directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly
collect personal information from children or adults. A parent or guardian with a privacy
question can contact us at the address below.

## Security

Local data is protected by Android's app sandbox and the security settings of your device.
No storage or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure, so keep your
device, operating system, and screen lock up to date.

## Changes to this policy

If Drawless Chess changes how it handles information, we will update this policy and its
"Last updated" date before or when the relevant app change is released. Material changes
will also be disclosed through the app or store listing when appropriate.
